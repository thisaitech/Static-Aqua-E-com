## Troubleshooting

### Problem: Invoice button not showing

**Solution:** Invoice button only appears for orders with "COMPLETED" payment status.

Check your order:
- Payment Status must be: **COMPLETED** âœ…
- If it shows "PENDING" ğŸŸ , run the SQL fix first

### Problem: Print button does nothing

**Solution:** Try the keyboard shortcut instead:
- Windows: Press **Ctrl+P**
- Mac: Press **Cmd+P**

### Problem: No "Save as PDF" option

**Solution:** Your browser might use different wording:
- Look for "Save as PDF"
- Or "Microsoft Print to PDF"
- Or "Print to PDF"

### Problem: Invoice opens but looks plain

**Solution:** That's normal! The styling is optimized for printing. When you save as PDF, it will look professional.
